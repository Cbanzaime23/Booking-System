1. Introduction

CCF Manila ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our Room Booking System.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of personal data necessary to process your room booking:

  • Full Name (to identify the booker)
  • Email Address (for booking confirmations and communication)
  • Booking Details (Event name, Date, Time, Room, Pax)

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To manage and schedule room reservations.
  • To send booking confirmations and updates.
  • To resolve scheduling conflicts.
  • For internal record-keeping and facility management.

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your booking data for a period of 1 year after the date of your event. After this period, your personal data will be automatically deleted from our records.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data before the retention period expires ("Right to be Forgotten").

To exercise these rights, you may use the "Request Data Deletion" feature on the home page or contact us directly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the CCF Manila Administration Office.
