Housekeeping Rules

By proceeding with this reservation, you agree to the following guidelines:

  • Exclusive Use: This system is for the exclusive use of CCF Manila ministries and discipleship groups.
  • Admin Overrides: Administrators reserve the right to manually reschedule or cancel bookings to prioritize official church events. You will be notified via email if such changes occur.
  • Grace Period: A 30-minute grace period is provided for each group to arrive. Bookings may be forfeited if the group fails to show up within this window.
  • Curfew: All meetings and events must conclude by 9:45 PM. Everyone must vacate the CCF Manila premises strictly before 10:00 PM.
  • Main Hall Tables: Specific table arrangements in the Main Hall may be adjusted by the housekeeping team depending on the number of participants.
  • Clean Up & Reset: You agree to clean up the venue after use and properly reset all chairs and equipment to their original arrangement.
  • Cancellation: Please cancel your reservation at least 24 hours in advance if your event cannot proceed to free up the room for others.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

This policy explains how CCF Manila collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the Reservation System.

1. Data We Collect

  • Name (first and last) — to identify your reservation
  • Email address — to send reservation confirmations and cancellation notices
  • Dgroup Leader Name — to verify that the user is a member of a Discipleship Group
  • Number of Participants — to manage room capacity
  • Notes — optional, provided at your discretion

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing and confirming your room reservation
  • Sending email notifications about your reservation status
  • Managing room availability and preventing scheduling conflicts
  • Contacting you if there are changes to your reservation

3. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained for 1 year after your reservation date. After this period, personal information is automatically anonymized. Anonymized reservation statistics may be kept for internal reporting.

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with any third parties. It is only accessible to authorized CCF Manila administrators for reservation management purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data via the "My Reservations" feature
  • Rectify incorrect data by contacting the admin
  • Delete your data by requesting erasure
  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Access to reservation records is restricted to authorized administrators only.

7. Contact

For questions about your personal data or to exercise your rights, contact us at: ccf manila admin
