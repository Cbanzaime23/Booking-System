Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

This policy explains how CCF Manila collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the Booking System.

1. Data We Collect

  • Name (first and last) — to identify your booking
  • Email address — to send booking confirmations and cancellation notices
  • Dgroup Leader Name — to verify that the user is a member of a Discipleship Group
  • Number of Participants — to manage room capacity
  • Notes — optional, provided at your discretion

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing and confirming your room booking
  • Sending email notifications about your booking status
  • Managing room availability and preventing scheduling conflicts
  • Contacting you if there are changes to your booking

3. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained for 1 year after your booking date. After this period, personal information is automatically anonymized. Anonymized booking statistics may be kept for internal reporting.

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with any third parties. It is only accessible to authorized CCF Manila administrators for booking management purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data via the "My Bookings" feature
  • Rectify incorrect data by contacting the admin
  • Delete your data by requesting erasure
  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Access to booking records is restricted to authorized administrators only.

7. Contact

For questions about your personal data or to exercise your rights, contact us at: ccf manila admin
