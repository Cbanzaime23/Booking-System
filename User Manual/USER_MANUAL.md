CCF Manila Room Reservation System - User Manual

For Regular Users

How to Book a Room

Select Room: Choose your desired room from the dropdown menu (top left).

Select Slot: Navigate to your desired date using the "Prev" and "Next" buttons. Click on a time slot that shows as "Available" or has "Spots left".

Fill Form:

Enter your Name and Email.

Dgroup Leader: Enter the name of your Dgroup leader.

Event: Select the type of event.

Time & Participants: Confirm the end time and number of people.

Confirm: Click "Confirm Booking". Review the summary details and click "Yes, Confirm".

Done: You will see a success message with a Booking Code. You will also receive a confirmation email.

Note: If you book a Mezzanine room but the Main Hall is available, the system may automatically move your booking to the Main Hall to optimize space. Your email will confirm which room you were assigned.

How to Cancel a Booking

Find the time slot of your booking on the calendar.

Click on the slot.

Select "Cancel a Booking".

Choose your booking from the list.

Enter the Email Address you used to make the booking.

Click "Confirm Cancellation".

For Administrators

Accessing the Dashboard

Go to the main reservation page.

Click the "Admin Dashboard" button in the top right corner.

Enter the secure Admin PIN.

Dashboard Features

Summary Cards: See total active bookings, counts per room, and bookings for today.

Charts: Visual breakdown of room usage and event types.

Gantt Chart: A timeline view showing exactly when rooms are occupied. Hover over bars to see details.

Room Tables: Detailed lists of every booking, separated by room.

Filters: Use the top bar to filter data by Specific Room or Specific Date.

Making an Admin Booking

Admins have special privileges when booking via the main calendar:

Open the booking form as usual.

Check the box "I am an Admin".

Enter the Admin PIN.

Extended Capabilities:

Advance Booking: You can book up to 6 months in advance (users are limited to 7 days).

Max Capacity: Select specific Ministry Events to auto-fill the room to maximum capacity (blocking it for others).

Recurrence: You can book repeating events (e.g., Weekly for 12 weeks, Monthly for 6 months).

Admin Cancellation

Admins can cancel any booking without knowing the user's email.

Click the booking slot.

Select "Cancel a Booking".

Choose the booking to cancel.

Enter the Admin PIN in the PIN field (leave email blank).

Click "Confirm Cancellation".
